About the Party

The Israel First Party is a new political movement built on a clear and simple idea: Israel must always act in the best interests of its citizens — their safety, prosperity, and future. We believe in responsible governance, economic strength, personal freedom, and a state that works for the people, not the other way around.

Our Platform

Security & Stability

Israel’s citizens deserve safety wherever they live. We support strong, decisive policies that protect Israelis and ensure long-term national stability.

Affordable Living

Housing costs are out of control. We propose real reforms to increase supply, cut bureaucracy, and allow young Israelis to build a future in this country.

Government That Works

Israel needs practical leadership, not endless political drama. We focus on effective solutions, transparency, and results for ordinary citizens.
